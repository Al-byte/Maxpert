The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

The Beast of Roots

A massive creature of vines erupted from the ground, forming a snarling beast with glowing green eyes. It roared and lunged. Maximus could have run. He could have fought. But he remembered what his father always taught him: “Bravery isn’t just facing danger — it’s choosing the right action in the face of it.” Maximus raised his hands. “I won’t harm you. Let me pass. I respect this forest.” The creature’s vines trembled… then lowered its head. The beast dissolved back into the earth, leaving the path open. Maximus exhaled shakily and moved forward.
