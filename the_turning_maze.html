The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

The Turning Maze

A maze of rotating vines and mirrors shifted constantly as Maximus entered it. A message appeared on the first mirror: “Your path reflects your mind.” When Maximus tensed, the walls closed in. When frustration flared, the pathways twisted apart. He closed his eyes and breathed deeply. He visualized calm; his father’s steady voice, quiet mornings, the hope of returning home a leader. The maze loosened, clearing a straight path forward. Maximus passed through with peaceful confidence.
