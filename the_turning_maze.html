The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

The Turning Maze

Without warning, thick vines erupted from the earth, twisting together into towering walls. Polished mirrors grew from between the vines, reflecting countless versions of Maximus as the maze sealed shut behind him. The moment he took a step forward, the walls shifted. A message burned into the surface of the nearest mirror:

Your path reflects your mind.

As tension crept into his chest, the corridors narrowed, pressing close. When frustration flared, paths split violently, pulling away from one another until no clear direction remained. Maximus stopped walking. He closed his eyes and forced himself to breathe slowly, deliberately. He thought of his father’s steady voice, of quiet mornings before responsibility weighed so heavily, of the purpose that waited beyond the forest.

Gradually, the maze responded. The vines loosened their grip. The mirrors steadied, reflecting not confusion, but resolve. A single, open path revealed itself. Maximus walked forward calmly, understanding now that leadership demanded mastery of the self before command of others.
