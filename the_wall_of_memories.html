The Wall of Memories

A shimmering wall reflected scenes from Maximus’ life: Times he failed. Times he succeeded. Times he felt unworthy of leadership. Words glowed above: “A leader learns. A leader lets go.” Maximus reached out and touched the wall. “I’m not perfect,” he said softly. “But I grow from every mistake. I won’t let my past failures rule my future.” The wall dissolved into drifting light. Maximus kept walking — head high.
