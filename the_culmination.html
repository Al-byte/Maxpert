The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

The Culmination

The chamber grew silent. The Keeper studied him for a long moment, then nodded.

“You may claim what lies inside.”

Behind him, a pedestal rose silently from the stone floor. Resting upon it was a small wooden box, carved from dark, time-worn wood whose grain told the story of age and endurance. Black iron bands wrapped around it like armor, their rivets and heavy lock giving the impression that what it held was not merely precious, but guarded by history itself—meant to be opened only by one proven worthy.

Maximus approached slowly. His hands trembled—not with fear, but with purpose.

“Rise, Guardian,” the Keeper said.

And Maximus stepped forward—no longer a boy, but a man prepared to LEAD.
