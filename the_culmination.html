The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

The Culmination

The chamber grew still. Then the Keeper nodded. “You may claim what lies inside.” From behind him, a pedestal rose from the floor. Upon it sat a small wooden box trimmed in gold and etched with ancient symbols. Maximus approached slowly, reverently. His hands shook — not with fear but with purpose — as he lifted the lid. A soft golden glow filled the room. Maximus’ eyes widened. But the audience never sees what he sees. The contents remained hidden, known only to Maximus — destined to shape not only his future, but the future of the entire society. Maximus closed the lid gently, holding the box to his chest. The Keeper bowed. “Rise, Guardian. Your journey begins now.” And Maximus stepped out of the chamber, no longer a boy. But a leader.
