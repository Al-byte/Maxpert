The Staircase of Light

A spiraling staircase of floating stone steps hovered in a column of golden haze. But the steps flickered, appearing and disappearing. A single line of text shimmered in the air: “Trust what you cannot see.” Maximus stepped boldly into the empty space — and a stone solidified beneath his foot. Another step. Another stone. He climbed upward, his trust unshaken, his spirit unafraid. At the top stood a final doorway of carved oak. Maximus pushed it open.
