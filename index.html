The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

In their dystopian district, leaders weren’t elected — they were proven. For generations, the future Guardians completed the Rite of the Rising, a journey of seven trials spread throughout Emberfall.

Only those who reached the final treasure and returned with it were trusted with guiding the people through famine, storms, and the dangers outside the barricades. And today, it was Maximus’ turn.

His father rested a hand on his shoulder. “Remember,” he said quietly, “it’s not strength that makes a leader, but the character behind it.” Maximus nodded. “I’ll return, Dad. And I’ll make you proud.”

Sixteen-year-old Maximus stood at the entrance to Emberfall Forest, the ancient wood that no one entered unless they were ready to accept the greatest responsibility their society could offer. With his father’s map, a small satchel, and a heart full of determination, Maximus stepped into the forest.
