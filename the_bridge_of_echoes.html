The Bridge of Echoes

A fragile bridge stretched across a massive ravine. As Maximus stepped forward, a shimmering version of himself appeared on the opposite side. The echo spoke. “Cross quickly, but only step where I have stepped.” The echo walked a zigzag pattern, fast and precise. Maximus closed his eyes and replayed it in his mind, marking each shift, each angle. His memory never failed him when he needed it most. He opened his eyes and crossed — one step at a time — careful, deliberate, exact. Wood creaked. Dust drifted into the abyss. But Maximus made it across without a single mistake.
