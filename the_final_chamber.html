The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

The Final Chamber

The chamber inside was vast and circular, lit by golden fire that cast no shadow. At its center stood a robed figure — the Keeper of Emberfall. “You have completed the seven trials,” the figure said. “But the true test remains.” Maximus lifted his chin. “What must I do?” “Tell me,” the Keeper said, “why you seek the treasure.” Maximus’ heart beat steadily. “Not for power,” he said. “Not for wealth. I seek it because my people need a leader who chooses integrity over strength, wisdom over fear, and compassion over dominance. This treasure isn’t for me — it’s for the future of our society. I want to protect them. Guide them. Honor the path of those before me… and prove myself worthy of walking it.”
