The Trials of Emberfall: Rite of the Rising

The Whispering Gate

A massive stone arch emerged from the shadows, its surface etched with glowing blue runes that pulsed like a living heartbeat. The path ended abruptly at its base. Whispers curled through the air, brushing against Maximus’ ears like unseen fingers.

“Speak the truth… or walk away.”

The runes shifted, rearranging themselves until a single question burned bright: What flaw lives in your heart?

Maximus felt heat rise in his chest. This was not a test of courage or cleverness. It demanded something far more dangerous—honesty. His instinct was to answer with something safe. Something noble. But the forest seemed to wait, listening. He swallowed hard.

“Sometimes,” he said slowly, “I feel pressure to become who everyone expects. And when I can’t measure up, I doubt myself.”

The whispers fell silent. The runes glowed warmly, their light softening from blue to gold. With a deep rumble, the archway split open. Maximus stepped through, understanding that leadership began with truth—especially truth about his own weaknesses.
